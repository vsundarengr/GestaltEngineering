To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, use the formula: \( F = \frac{9}{5}C + 32 \). Substitute \( C = {{params.tempC}} \) into the formula to get the equivalent in Fahrenheit. The equivalent of {{params.tempC}}°C in Fahrenheit is \( F = \frac{9}{5} \times {{params.tempC}} + 32 = {{correct_answers.tempF}}°F \).

First, draw all of the reaction forces acting on all of the bearings.

The reaction force on bearing A should be $A_x$ and $A_z$

The reaction forces on bearing B should be $B_x$ and $B_z$

The reaction forces on bearing C should be $C_y$ and $C_z$

Now write out the equations that discribe the force in all directions and moments in all directions.
    Equations that discribe all of the forces:
  • $\Sigma{F}_x = 0 \implies A_x + B_x -F_R = 0$
  • $\Sigma{F}_y = 0 \implies C_y - F_G =0$
  • $\Sigma{F}_z = 0 \implies A_z + B_z + C_z - F_B -F_P =0$
    • Moment equations (about point A):
  • $\Sigma{M}_x = 0 \implies (B_z)(L_1+L_1)+(C_z)(L_1+L_1+L_1)+(F_G)(L_2)-(F_P)(L_1+L_1+L_1)-(F_B)(L_1)=0$
  • $\Sigma{M}_y = 0 \implies (C_z)(L_1+L_1)-(F_P)(L_1)+(F_B)(L_2) =0$
  • $\Sigma{M}_z = 0 \implies (F_R)(L_1)-(B_x)(L_1+L_1)+(F_G)(L_1)-(C_y)(L_1+L_1)=0$
    • Now you have 6 equations and 6 unknowns. Now we can solve for everthing.
